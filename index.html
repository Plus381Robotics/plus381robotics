About Us

Welcome to our robotics team page! We are a passionate group of engineers and enthusiasts dedicated to exploring the world of robotics. Our mission is to design, build and innovate robots for European competition Eurobot. The main goal is to support students in creating and designing robots while following trends and implementing new technologies. Throughout our 3 years of work we gained support from the Faculty of Technical Sciences in Novi Sad and several companies who were providing assistance.
