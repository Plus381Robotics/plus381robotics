Fundamentals

Discover the cutting-edge technologies and knowledge our team employs, from advanced robotics and AI algorithms to innovative programming languages and electronic components, all working in harmony to bring our robotic creations to life. We utilize a variety of advanced technologies to excel in the fields of electronics, mechanics, and programming.

Mechanics

In mechanics, we leverage Autodesk Fusion 360 for 3D modeling and simulation, allowing us to develop intricate mechanical components and assemblies.

Electronics

For electronics, we use KiCad to design circuit boards for precise layouts. Altium enhances our PCB design process and streamlines collaboration, while LTspice helps us simulate and analyze analog circuits, optimizing performance and ensuring reliability.

Programming

On the programming side, we rely on STM32CubeIDE for embedded system development. Through these technologies, we implement classical mechanisms and algorithms, ensuring robust and reliable robotic solutions.
